Our Medical Departments

Expert care in every specialty, from general medicine to advanced surgery and critical care.

General Medicine

Department of General Medicine deals with very simple to very intricate ailments, providing proper examination, immunization, assessment and treatment in all conditions by a team of specialized, well-experienced medical professionals. This department specializes in diabetes type one & two. Diabetes is treated not only with medication but a thorough observation & guidelines are given related to diet & overall lifestyle change to control and help reverse diabetes.

General & Laparoscopic Surgery

Our skilled surgeons specialize in laparoscopic procedures, which reduce pain, minimize scarring, and promote faster recovery for patients. We offer comprehensive surgical care from routine to advanced procedures.

Cardiology

Our team of experienced cardiologists uses state-of-the-art technology for accurate assessments, including ECG, echocardiography, stress tests, and advanced cardiac imaging.

Neurology & Neurosurgery

Our team of highly skilled neurologists and neurosurgeons uses advanced diagnostic tools such as MRI, CT scans, EEG, and neuro-monitoring systems to ensure precise diagnosis and treatment.

Orthopedics

Our experienced orthopedic surgeons provide care for bone fractures, joint disorders, ligament injuries, arthritis, and sports-related injuries, using advanced surgical techniques and modern orthopedic technology.

Obstetrics & Gynecology

Our experienced team of obstetricians and gynecologists specializes in prenatal care, safe delivery, high-risk pregnancies, infertility management, and routine gynecological care.

Pediatrics & Neonatology

Our team of experienced pediatricians and neonatologists provides preventive care, immunizations, treatment of common and complex childhood illnesses, and specialized neonatal support.

Dermatology

Our experienced dermatologists specialize in diagnosing and treating acne, eczema, psoriasis, fungal infections, pigmentation disorders, hair loss, and skin allergies, as well as performing advanced cosmetic and dermatological procedures.

Ophthalmology (Eye Care)

Our team of experienced ophthalmologists provides diagnosis, treatment, and management of eye conditions including cataracts, glaucoma, refractive errors, diabetic retinopathy, and corneal disorders.

ENT

Our team of experienced ENT specialists treats sinus disorders, hearing loss, throat infections, tonsillitis, sleep apnea, and voice disorders, using advanced diagnostic tools and modern treatment techniques.

Dental & Maxillofacial Surgery

Our team of skilled dentists and oral surgeons specializes in routine dental treatments, root canal therapy, cosmetic dentistry, wisdom tooth removal, jaw surgeries, and corrective facial procedures.

Urology

Our experienced urologists specialize in treating kidney stones, urinary tract infections, prostate disorders, bladder issues, and male infertility, using advanced diagnostic tools and minimally invasive surgical techniques.

Radiology & Pathology

Our state-of-the-art imaging facilities include X-ray, CT Scan, MRI, Ultrasound, and Digital Imaging, enabling precise diagnosis for a wide range of medical conditions.

Special Services

Round-the-clock emergency care, critical care units, and advanced diagnostic support.

24/7 Emergency Care

Immediate care and ambulance dispatch when you need us most. Our emergency department is staffed round the clock with experienced doctors and support staff to handle all urgent medical needs.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Dedicated critical care with ventilator support. Our ICU is equipped with advanced monitoring systems and staffed by intensivists and trained nurses for patients requiring round-the-clock critical care.

NICU & PICU

Neonatal and pediatric intensive care. We provide specialized care for critically ill newborns and children in a safe, family-friendly environment with the latest equipment and expert staff.

Modular Operation Theatres

State-of-the-art OTs for zero infection risk. Our modular operation theatres are designed to maintain the highest standards of asepsis and are equipped with advanced surgical equipment for a wide range of procedures.

Dialysis & Critical Support

Renal care and life-saving interventions. We offer hemodialysis and support for patients with kidney-related conditions, along with comprehensive critical care for those requiring intensive medical support.

X-Ray, CT Scan & Ultrasound

Digital imaging and advanced diagnostics. Our radiology department offers X-Ray, CT Scan, and Ultrasound services with modern equipment and expert radiologists for accurate, timely diagnosis.

Lab & Diagnostic Services

Automated pathology and quick results. Our laboratory is equipped with automated systems for blood tests, cultures, and other diagnostics, ensuring reliable results and faster turnaround for treatment decisions.
