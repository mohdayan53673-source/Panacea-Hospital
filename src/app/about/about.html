Healing Hands, Compassionate Hearts

Founded on the principles of selfless service and medical excellence, Panacea Hospital & Research Center has been a beacon of hope and healing in Amroha for over two decades. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful belief: every individual deserves access to quality healthcare.

Our journey has been driven by a singular focus—to combine cutting-edge medical technology with unwavering compassion. Today, we stand as a trusted multi-speciality institution, committed to setting new standards in patient care, research, and community health.
